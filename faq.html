Frequently asked question

What classes are available in the school?
The school provides education from LKG to Class 10th. We focus on building a strong academic foundation along with discipline and overall personality development of students.
When was the school established?
Madhusudan Bal Vidhya Secondary School Mungthala was established in 2004. Since then, the school has been committed to providing quality education and moral values to students.
Is the school co-educational?
Yes, the school is a co-educational institution where both boys and girls study together. We believe in providing equal learning opportunities to every student.
What is the medium of instruction?
The primary medium of instruction in the school is Hindi. Teachers use simple and effective teaching methods for better understanding of students.
How can I apply for admission?
Parents can apply for admission by visiting the school campus or filling out the admission form available on the website. The admission process is simple and student-friendly.
What documents are required for admission?
Documents such as birth certificate, previous class marksheet, passport-size photographs, and transfer certificate are generally required during admission.
Does the school provide transport facilities?
The school may provide transport facilities depending on the student’s location and route availability. Parents can contact the school office for detailed information.
What are the school timings?
School timings are decided according to the academic schedule and seasonal requirements. Students are expected to maintain punctuality and regular attendance.
Are extracurricular activities available?
Yes, the school encourages students to participate in extracurricular activities such as dance, music, sports, cultural programs, and competitions for overall development.
Does the school organize sports competitions?
Yes, sports competitions and physical activities are organized regularly to promote teamwork, fitness, discipline, and confidence among students.
How does the school focus on student discipline?
The school strongly believes in discipline, moral values, and respectful behavior. Students are guided to become responsible and confident individuals.
Are experienced teachers available in the school?
Yes, the school has qualified and experienced teachers who are dedicated to providing quality education and proper guidance to students.
Does the school prepare students for competitive exams?
Yes, students are encouraged and guided for competitive examinations and future academic goals through proper support and motivation.
How can parents contact the school administration?
Parents can contact the school administration through phone calls, email, or by visiting the school office during working hours.
Does the school celebrate cultural and national events?
Yes, the school actively celebrates cultural programs, annual functions, and national festivals to encourage creativity, patriotism, and student participation.
How are students encouraged in personality development?
Students are encouraged through activities, public speaking, competitions, leadership programs, and teamwork opportunities for personality development.
Are digital learning methods used in classrooms?
Yes, the school uses modern teaching methods and digital learning techniques whenever required to improve the learning experience of students.
Does the school provide a safe learning environment?
Yes, the school provides a safe, positive, and student-friendly environment where students can learn comfortably and confidently.
What facilities are available on the school campus?
The school campus includes classrooms, activity areas, playground facilities, and a positive environment that supports both academics and extracurricular activities.
Why should parents choose our school for their children?
The school focuses on quality education, discipline, moral values, and overall student growth. We aim to build confident, knowledgeable, and responsible citizens for the future.

About Us

MADHUSUDAN BAL VIDYA MANDIR MUNGTHALA was established in 2004 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located at Rural area of ABU-ROAD block of SIROHI district of Rajasthan. The school consists of Grades from Nursary to 10. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Non-Ashram type (Govt.) in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
