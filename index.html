About Us

Why We Are Better

Madhusudan Bal Vidya Mandir Mungthala was established in 2004 and is managed by a Private Unaided Management.

Where the School is located at?
The school is located in the rural area of Abu Road Block in the Sirohi District of Rajasthan.
What is the mission of the school?
The mission of the school is to provide affordable and quality education for a brighter future of students.
What is the achievement of the school?
By the academic session 2025–26, the school has successfully helped more than 50 students secure admission in Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) across the district, which is considered the highest selection record achieved by any school in the entire district.
What are the main objectives and focus areas of the school?
The school focuses not only on academics but also on discipline, moral values, personality development, and competitive preparation of students.

About Us

MADHUSUDAN BAL VIDYA MANDIR MUNGTHALA was established in 2004 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located at Rural area of ABU-ROAD block of SIROHI district of Rajasthan. The school consists of Grades from Nursary to 10. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Non-Ashram type (Govt.) in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Hindi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
