About Us

MADHUSUDAN BAL VIDYA MANDIR MUNGTHALA was established in 2004 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located at Rural area of ABU-ROAD block of SIROHI district of Rajasthan. The school consists of Grades from Nursery to 10. The school is Co-educational and it has an attached pre-primary section. Hindi is the medium of instruction in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
